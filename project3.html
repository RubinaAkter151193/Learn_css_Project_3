Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This Apple pie recipie has been in my family for generations & never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinamon
  • 6 mediumsize-d Apple, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Prehit oven to 375 degree F(190 degree C)
  2. In a bowl,mis the sugar & cinnamon
  3. Toss the Apple in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
